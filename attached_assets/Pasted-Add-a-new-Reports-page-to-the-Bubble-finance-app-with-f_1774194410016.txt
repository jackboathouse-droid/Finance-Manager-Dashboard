Add a new "Reports" page to the Bubble finance app with full financial reporting functionality.

---

BACKEND:

Create API endpoints to support reports:

1. /reports/profit-loss
- Returns total income, total expenses, and net profit
- Grouped by month and by category

2. /reports/balance-sheet
- Returns:
  Assets (bank accounts)
  Liabilities (credit cards)
  Net worth
- Calculated from transactions

3. /reports/spending
- Returns spending breakdown by category and subcategory

4. All reports must support filters:
- date range (start_date, end_date)
- category_id
- account_id
- person

---

LOGIC RULES:

- Expenses = negative values
- Income = positive values
- Transfers must be excluded from P&L
- Balance sheet:
  Assets = sum of bank accounts
  Liabilities = sum of credit cards
  Net worth = Assets - Liabilities

---

FRONTEND:

1. Add "Reports" to sidebar navigation

2. Reports Page Layout:

Top section:
- Filters:
  - Date range picker
  - Category filter
  - Account filter
  - Person filter
  - Search bar (global search across transactions and reports)

---

SECTION 1: Profit & Loss

- Show:
  Total Income
  Total Expenses
  Net Profit

- Chart:
  Line chart showing income vs expenses over time

- Table:
  Breakdown by category

---

SECTION 2: Balance Sheet

- Show:
  Assets (grouped by account)
  Liabilities (grouped by account)
  Net Worth

- Chart:
  Bar chart comparing assets vs liabilities

---

SECTION 3: Spending Analysis

- Pie chart:
  Spending by category

- Bar chart:
  Spending by subcategory

---

SECTION 4: Detailed Report Table

- Table of transactions
- Fully searchable and filterable
- Columns:
  Date, Description, Category, Subcategory, Account, Amount

---

SEARCH FUNCTIONALITY:

- Add a global search bar at the top of the Reports page
- User can type anything (e.g. "groceries", "March", "Visa")
- Dynamically filter:
  - charts
  - tables
  - metrics

---

UX REQUIREMENTS:

- Clean, modern fintech UI (consistent with Bubble theme)
- Use cards for each report section
- Smooth loading states
- Responsive design
- Charts update instantly when filters change

---

GOAL:

Create a fully interactive Reports page that behaves like professional accounting software, allowing users to explore their financial data through charts, tables, and filters in real time
