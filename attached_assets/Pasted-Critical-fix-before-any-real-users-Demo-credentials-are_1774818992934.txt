Critical — fix before any real users
Demo credentials are hardcoded and publiccritical
admin@bubble.app / admin is seeded in seed.ts and visible in your public GitHub repo. Anyone can log in to your live app right now.
Registration cap of 5 users (MAX_USERS)critical
Your replit.md documents MAX_USERS env var defaulting to 5. A SaaS that stops letting people sign up after 5 users cannot grow.
express-session without a persistent storecritical
Sessions are stored in memory by default in Express. Every server restart (Replit sleeps!) logs out all users. This is not acceptable for a production app.
No HTTPS enforcement or security headerscritical
Missing Helmet.js means no CSP, no HSTS, no X-Frame-Options. A finance app without these headers fails basic security audits.
No GDPR / PIPEDA compliance layercritical
You handle personal financial data for UK and Canadian users. You legally need a Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and data deletion mechanism before onboarding real users.
Important — needed for SaaS
No Stripe billing — zero revenue pathimportant
The app has no free tier, paid tier, trial, or billing management. You cannot charge anyone.
No bank connectivity (Flinks / TrueLayer)important
Users must manually enter every transaction. Without open banking you cannot compete with Emma, Cleo, or Mint.
Server sleeps (Replit free tier)important
Replit free projects sleep after ~5 minutes of inactivity. A finance app going offline randomly is a trust-killer.
No email notificationsimportant
No welcome email, password reset email, weekly digest, or budget alerts — all of which are expected features.
No onboarding flowimportant
New users land on a blank dashboard. Activation (users getting value) requires connecting a bank or adding data — currently there is zero guidance.
Categories are global, not user-editableimportant
Your categories and subcategories tables have no user_id — they're a shared global taxonomy. Users cannot add custom categories.
